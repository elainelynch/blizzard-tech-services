Our Services

Cleaning & Maintenance

Includes general cleaning of evaporator and condenser. We provide cleaning and preventive maintenance services to keep your air conditioning units in tip-top shape throughout the years to come.

Check-up & Repair

If you're having sudden problems with your aircon units, we're here to help. Let our professional technicians find the cause of the problem and provide a solution to get back your aircon units in their normal operation.

Installation

From a simple mounting of your window-type air conditioner to the installation of large split-type systems. Our TESDA Certified and National Certificate (NC) holder technicians provide excellent and professional services for all your airconditioning installation needs.

Dismantling & Relocation

We provide a safe dismantling of your air conditioning units and relocating them to your new desired location. Let our professional technicians do the job.
